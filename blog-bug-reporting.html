Common Bug Reporting Mistakes

Author: Saurabh Das | QA Lead

Clear and well-structured bug reports help developers quickly understand and fix issues.

Common Mistakes

  • Missing reproduction steps
  • Unclear expected vs actual results
  • No screenshots or logs
  • Vague bug titles

How to Write a Good Bug Report

  1. Write a clear bug title
  2. Provide exact reproduction steps
  3. Include expected vs actual results
  4. Attach screenshots or logs
  5. Mention environment details

Well-written bug reports improve team efficiency and speed up the debugging process.
