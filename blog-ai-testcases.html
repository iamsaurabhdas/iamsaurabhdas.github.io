Using AI to Generate Test Cases

Author: Saurabh Das

Artificial Intelligence is transforming many industries, including software testing. AI-powered tools can assist QA engineers in generating test scenarios faster.

Benefits of AI in Testing

  • Faster test case generation
  • Better edge case detection
  • Improved test coverage

How I Use AI in QA

I experiment with AI tools to generate test case ideas and improve test documentation. However, manual review is always required to ensure business logic is correct.

Limitations

  • AI may miss domain-specific scenarios
  • Generated tests require human validation

AI should be considered a helpful assistant rather than a replacement for human testers.
