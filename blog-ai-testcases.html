Using AI to Generate Test Cases

Author: Saurabh Das | QA Lead

Artificial Intelligence is increasingly helping QA engineers improve productivity. AI tools can assist testers in generating test case ideas and identifying edge scenarios.

Benefits of AI in Testing

  • Faster test case generation
  • Improved test coverage
  • Better edge case discovery

How I Use AI in QA

AI tools help generate initial test scenarios which I review and refine manually based on business requirements.

Limitations

  • AI may miss domain-specific cases
  • Human validation is always required

AI should be considered a productivity assistant rather than a replacement for human testers.
